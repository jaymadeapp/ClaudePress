## Re-grading / replacing

The warm grade is a subtle, reversible color treatment (gentle desaturation + warm
white-balance shift + a low-opacity clay-tint colorize). To swap in client photos,
drop replacements at the same filenames; the theme's `customDuotone` warm-clay preset
will keep client-swapped photos on-brand without manual grading.
